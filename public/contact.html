Julia Weppler is a 28 years old photographer from Cologne (Germany) who is currently in her last year of study at the Folkwang University of Arts.

She collaborates with companies, agencies and individuals in order to carry out their editorial portraits, fashion shootings, interior photography and weddings.

In addition to her commercial work she practices artistic photography by combining shapes and colors in detailed and sensitive ways. Her goal is to experiment with the forms of the human body in combination with textiles, objects and their general surroundings. The boundary between fashion and art photography is fluid and intended in her work and doesn’t stand as a contradiction to her. Influenced by the knowledge granted by her degree in psychology she makes her work go beyond surfaces and is questioning and working with standards and norms that are shaping our society and us.
